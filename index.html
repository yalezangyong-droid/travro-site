About

Built by someone who knows both sides.

Most luggage brands are built by marketers who found a manufacturer. TRAVRO is built by someone who grew up in the industry—who spent years on both sides of the factory floor and the business class cabin.

After years in global logistics and supply chain at Maersk, then an MBA at Yale, our founder Will Zang kept asking the same question: why does everyone in the know buy their luggage from the factory direct, while everyone else pays five times the price for a label?

TRAVRO is the answer. Not a brand that happens to make luggage. A manufacturing team that finally decided to talk directly to the people who carry it.

Questions

What you're probably wondering.

Kickstarter operates on an all-or-nothing model: if we don't reach our funding goal, you're not charged. We're confident — but your money is always protected. No goal met means no charge, no risk.

We're targeting delivery within 4–6 months of a successful campaign close. Our manufacturing partners are already tooled and ready — this isn't a design project looking for funding. It's a factory-ready product launching direct to consumers for the first time.

The K35 shell is high-impact polycarbonate — the same material found in premium luggage at 3–5× the price. The lining is 210D ripstop nylon. Wheels are Hinomoto precision spinners from Japan. Zippers are YKK RCSS. TSA-approved combination lock is built-in standard.

Yes. The K35 collapses from a full 21″ carry-on to just 4 inches deep using a hinged internal frame. It takes about 10 seconds. Folded, it stores flat under a bed, behind a door, or in a closet — no wasted space at home, full carry-on volume when you travel.

The K35 carries a 5-year manufacturing warranty covering defects in materials and workmanship. The LK001 Aluminum Frame carries a lifetime warranty. We stand behind our manufacturing — it's all we do.

The K35 meets carry-on size requirements for all major airlines when fully expanded (21″ × 14″ × 9″). We recommend verifying your specific airline's policy, especially for international or budget carriers with stricter limits.

TRAVRO is a brand of Travel Trail Goods Inc., based in California. The K35 is manufactured by our long-term partners in Asia — the same factories that produce luggage for major premium brands. We're not hiding that. It's the whole point.
